Company Overview

Chakkery Engineering Equipments

Chakkery Engineering Equipments is a well established industrial unit in the making of delicate machineries used in water and waste water treatment plants.

Founded by the ace visionary Mr.C.A.Andrews way back in 1989, the unit has already completed 35 years of successful operation. In a span of two and a half decades we have installed a good number of water and waste water treatment plants in various large scale as well as small sale factories and hospitals in and out of Kerala states.

Our Products

Fixed aerator

Fixed Type Surface
Aerator

Fixed aerator in 0.5HP – 20HP power range is designed so as to work smoothly at a fixed water level. These aerators are made of mild steel or stainless steel as per the requirement of the client. FRP lining can be done on the fixed aerator made of mild steel.

Floating aerators

Floating Type Surface
Aerator

Floating aerators, in the power range of 1.5 HP – 20HP are constructed with high quality FRP materials and are designed so as to work in variable water level. Rotating parts and the parts of the aerator in contact with water are made of stainless steel Since these aerators have heavy thrust bearing systems they work quite free of trouble for 24 hours.

Stirrers
